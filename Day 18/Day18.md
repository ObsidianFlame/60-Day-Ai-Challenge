Build a Brain Dump Action Planner Skill
Transform chaos into clarity using Claude Skills

What i did:
Custom Skills allow Claude to remember complex workflows. 
Instead of manually organizing notes every time, you can create a reusable skill that automatically transforms unstructured information into project dashboards, action plans, and decision logs.

Today we used the brain dump action planner to sort through random notes taken during seminars, meetings etc. and place them in a structured format to be utilised through a brief, summary and action plans.
My raw notes were from the TiE Dubai Event on Adaptive intelligence.
After building the claude skill, i pasted raw notes and got an output that I wanted, with the skill going further than the original to research topics to flesh them out, and provide an action plan to improve my 'adaptive intelligence.'

What i Learned:
The output was an intelligence brief for the TiE Dubai Event, the notes i took and what i learned.
I pulled supporting data from credible sources like Gallup and some online resources.
Today I applied the Brain Dump Action Planner skill to two very different inputs: a personal fragrance collection and raw notes from a TiE Dubai event on Adaptive Intelligence. 
The key insight was that a well-structured skill doesn't care what you feed it — it imposes the same rigour on fragrance organisation as it does on leadership theory. 
For the event notes, I added a research prompt that directed Claude to expand the raw points with external evidence, producing a published intelligence brief rather than a cleaned-up note. 
That brief then became the source material for a LinkedIn post — meaning one Claude session generated three distinct outputs: a structured dashboard, a research-expanded brief, and publishable content. 
The learning: custom skills are multipliers. 
You define the workflow once; the output adapts to whatever the input is.
