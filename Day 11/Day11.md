ATS Resume Optimizer & Resume Generator

What I did:
ATS systems act as the first screening layer for many companies. 
Understanding how resumes are evaluated helps candidates improve visibility, keyword alignment, and recruiter readability without exaggerating or inventing information.
Today I was tasked with building an ATS Resume Optimizer and Resume Generator.

What I learned:
I am in the job market currently, this challenge showed that a resume is not meant to be a long-winded set of achievements since childhood, it should be clear, succinct and aligned to the role you seek. 
Through context-specific prompting, i produced an ATS-optimized resume. I learned that the prompt needs to include specific instructions such as 'maintain 100% accuracy,' something you'd think was a given, but AI hallucinations are real, and job searches are serious business. It is best to leave nothing up to interpretation.
I learned that there is such a thing as 'ATS-friendly' formatting. My previous job searches have been very straightforward compared to the new way of seeking a job that involves AI, automation and machines that decide whether your resume is even worth looking at.
The outcome of the ATS Resume optimiser was the production of an Overall Match score, based on strict guidelines that included different categories. The optimiser included scoring rationale, category breakdown, gap analysis and an optimized resume, among others.
Some key insights include that I often apply for roles that stretch my skillset, indicating I prefer a challenge. Clinical Credibility is the rarest asset in Healthcare AI customer success.
As Medical Affairs undergoes digital transformation like real-time insights, AI-driven HCP personalisation, digital medical education — early, verifiable AI adopters represent a structural differentiation advantage that pharma-track candidates rarely possess.

How I will use this:
As I apply for jobs, I will use the optimizer to assess the fit for jobs and pursue those where there is a strong fit of 65% and above, indicating a good chance of landing an interview.
